ABOUT US

Established in 2008, Blooms Green Farm is one among the few establishments aimed at bringing the world closer to the pristine wonders of Wayanad. It was built on the foundations of organic farming and peaceful co-existence with Mother Nature. Although started in 1979 with organic farms, the company has branched into homemade products, resorts, real estate development and kennels.
